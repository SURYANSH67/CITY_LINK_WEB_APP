About CityLink Connect

Bridging Citizens and Local Governance for a Better Tomorrow

Our Mission

CityLink Connect is a citizen-first civic-tech platform designed to promote transparency, accountability, and community participation. Our goal is to empower residents to report non-emergency civic issues efficiently and to enable government departments to respond swiftly and transparently. Together, we aim to build a smarter, safer, and more sustainable city.

How It Works

1. Submit a Report

Use our intuitive online form to report issues such as potholes, garbage dumping, or damaged infrastructure. Add a location, description, and image if available.

2. Review & Assign

Each complaint is assigned a tracking ID and routed to the appropriate municipal department for action, ensuring timely and structured follow-up.

3. Track Progress

Monitor the status of your report in real-time—from submission to resolution—through your unique tracking ID on the platform.

CityLink Connect is a civic service powered by the Nagar Palika Parishad Sitapur, Department of Public Works to enhance responsive governance and public engagement.

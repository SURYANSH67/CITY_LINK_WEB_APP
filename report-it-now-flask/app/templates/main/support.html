Frequently Asked Questions

This portal is for non-emergency civic issues such as potholes, broken streetlights, graffiti, trash overflow, or damaged public property. **For emergencies, please dial your local emergency number immediately.**

Currently, the Ticket ID is the only way to track a report publicly. This is to protect your privacy. If you provided an email address during submission, all status updates will be sent to you automatically. If you have lost your ID and did not provide an email, you may need to contact us directly with the location and date of your report.

Resolution times vary depending on the complexity, severity, and resources required for the issue. You can monitor the status on the "Track Report" page. The status will change from "Pending" to "In Progress" once a team has been assigned, and finally to "Resolved" when the work is complete.
